Fabricae ex Nihilo with EMI

Fabricae ex Nihilo is a minecraft mod that adds ways to obtain items that are normally non renewable in vanilla. Emi is a minecraft mod that lets you view all item recipes and build a calculation tree that tells you the amount of resources needed to make a batch of items. I have written a mod in scala that adds compatibility between these two mods, so you can view Fabricaie Ex Nihilo recipes in EMI.

I also have submitted minor bug fixes to Fabricae ex Nihilo.

This is similar to my Compact Machines mod. They are both minecraft mods, but one has a wider scope.
